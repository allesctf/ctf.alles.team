ALLES! CTF 2021

is looking forward to host this year's ALLES! CTF.

When?

Begin: Fri, 03 Sep 2021 16:00 UTC
End: Sun, 05 Sep 2021 4:00 UTC (36 hours)

Where?

The CTF is open to everyone and can be played online. You do not need to pre-register for the event. A CTFtime.org account will be required to sign into the event (make sure your team members have access!).

IRC channel for this event is #allesctf on irc.hackint.org ( WebIRC).

What?

We prepared typical challenges in the classic categories (Reversing, Forensics, Crypto, Web, Pwning, Devops, and Misc), plus some weird stuff you didn't expect! If you have played last year's ALLES! CTF 2020, you know what to expect.

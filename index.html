ALLES! CTF 2021

is looking forward to host this year's ALLES! CTF.

When?

Begin: Fri, 03 Sep 2021 16:00 UTC
End: Sun, 05 Sep 2021 4:00 UTC (36 hours)

Where?

The CTF is open to everyone and can be played online. You do not need to pre-register for the event. A CTFtime.org account will be required to sign into the event (make sure your team members have access!).

Communication for this event will happen on Discord.

What?

We prepared typical challenges in the classic categories (Reversing, Forensics, Crypto, Web, Pwning, Devops, and Misc), plus some weird stuff you didn't expect! If you have played last year's ALLES! CTF 2020, you know what to expect.

Prize Pool

We're happy to announce that this year's ALLES!-CTF will have a very generous prize pool of $35,000, sponsored by our friends over at the Solana Foundation. $25,000 are allocated to the top 10 in the following way:

  9. $1,000
  10. $1,000

We will also give $100 each to the 20 best writeups we receive after the event. Send us your writeups to allesctfteam+writeups@gmail.com until Sun, 19 Sep 2021 anywhere on earth. We only accept pdf files.

We have prepared 4 challenges that are especially challenging and will require a large amount of effort to solve. For each of these challenges, if the challenge has 20 solvers or less, $2,000 will be distributed among all solvers after the event is over.

Prizes will be paid out as USDC. Because we don't want to pay hundreds of dollars in transaction fees, we will distribute them on the Solana blockchain. You can either directly deposit your SPL-USDC on FTX, or trade them to SOL (which you can then deposit on any major exchange) on any Solana DEX or swap, like for example Raydium or Serum. We will also send 0.0001 SOL to any address that receives prize money, in order to cover any transaction fees that might apply while getting the funds onto an exchange. This is enough for 20 transactions.

In order to be eligible for any prize money, you will have to send your Team token, which is displayed on the CTF platform, to allesctfteam+kyc@gmail.com until Sun, 12 Sep 2021 anywhere on earth. Please also include the public key of a Solana account that you have access to. We recommend you use a wallet like Phantom or Solflare as both of those natively support SPL-token transfers and have integrations with most onchain exchanges and swaps.
